Homemade Mayonnaise

Serving Size

Ingredients

Homemade Mayonnaise

  • 1 egg yolk
  • ½tsp salt
  • 1tbsp vinegar
  • 150cc salad oil

Sesame Mayo Dressing

  • 60-80g mayonnaise
  • 3tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2tbsp mirin
  • 1½tsp soy sauce
  • 1tsp vinegar
  • ½tsp sesame oil

Japanese Style Salad Dressing

  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • ½tbsp vinegar
  • 1tbsp mirin
  • ½tbsp sesame oil

Japanese Style Green Onion Salad Dressing

  • ¼ green onion
  • ½tsp salt

Steps

Homemade Mayonnaise

  1. Mix the salt and the egg yolk
  2. Add the vinegar to Step 1 and mix until the salt has completely dissolved
  3. Mix Step 2 well while gradually adding the salad oil. Whisk until the mixture has emulsified and peaks form

Sesame Mayo Dressing

  1. Simmer the mirin until about half is left. Remove from heat and let cool
  2. Crush the sesame seeds. Mix the seeds, Step 1, soy sauce, vinegar and sesame oil together
  3. Add mayonnaise to Step 2 until desired consistently

Japanese Style Salad Dressing

  1. Simmer the mirin then let cool
  2. Combine all the ingredients together

Japanese Style Green Onion Saald Dressing

  1. Dice the green onion into tiny pieces, add salt and mix
  2. Press Step 1 through a sieve until all the slime has been removed. Mix Step 2 well while gradually adding the salad oil. Whisk until the mixture has emulsified and peaks form. Rinse the sieve in some water, then dry.
  3. Mix Step 2 to the Japanese Dressing
    4. You can also mix in (1 bulb of) Japanese ginger to the Japanese dressing!
  4. Season with (a tiny amount) of salt
