Egg Sandwich

Makes 2-3

Ingredients

Egg Sandwich

  • 4 slices bread (from a loaf of bred with 10-12 slices)
  • 2 eggs
  • 20g onion
  • 15-20g butter
  • ¼tsp salt
  • 1tbsp Japanese mayo

Guacamole Sandwich

  • 6 slices bread (from a loaf of bread with 10-12 slices)
  • 1 (200g) avocado
  • ¼ tomato
  • 20-30g butter
  • 1tbsp lime juice
  • some salt

  • Group A

    • ⅛ (25g) onion
    • ½ clove garlic
    • ½ green chili
    • 1tsp minced cilantro

Steps

Egg Sandwich

  1. Warm butter to room temperature
  2. Add salt (separate from that in ingredients) to some boiling water, then boil egg for 10 minutes and peel shell.
  3. Mince onion and add salt
  4. Let the onion sit for a minute then wrap in cloth, massage, and squeeze
  5. Pour running water on the onions to get the salt off, then squeeze once more
  6. Use egg cutter or knife to mince the boiled egg then add the onions and mayo and mix
  7. Coat the bread with butter and use it to sandwhich the egg mixture
  8. Cut into desired size and you're done!

Guacamole Sandwich

  1. Warm butter to room temperature
  2. Make a cut along the avocado in a vertical line, place the knife against the pit
  3. Hold each side and twist to sesparate the two halves
  4. Stab the seed with the tip of the tknife and swist to remove the pit
  5. Use a spoon to remove the flesh, then lightly mash it with a masher
  6. Add lime juice and mix. This stops it from changing color. You can also put the avocado pit in the mix to do this!
  7. Scoop out the part of the tomato with the seeds, then chop up the rest of the tomato
  8. Mince all ingredients in Group A
  9. Mix everything from Step 6, Step 7, and Step 8 with the lime juice
  10. Butter the bread and then put the mixture between slices
  11. Cut into desired size and you're done!
