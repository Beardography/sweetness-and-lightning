Sesame Short Bread

Makes 30

Ingredients

Sesame Short Bread

  • 75g flour
  • 50g butter

  • Group A

    • 25g raw sugar
    • 10g white sesame

Boule De Neige

  • 60g flour
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 25g almond powder
  • 15g sugar

Steps

Sesame Short Bread

  1. Let the butter come to room temperature
  2. Sift the flour into a bowl and add the butter from Step 1. Cut and combine the flour and butter until it becomes similar to bread crumbs. Once combined add Group A
  3. Fold and mix the dough until it becomes uniform. As it begins to take shape mold it into forms
    4. It's okay if it falls apart a little at this point!
  4. Cover the top with cellophane and spread the dough, pushing down hard with a table spoon
  5. Remove the cellophane, bake in pre-heated oven at 150°C for 40 minutes
  6. Once baked, score with a knife and leave to cool. Enjoy once it's completely cooled

Boule De Neige

  1. Bring butter to room temperature
  2. Add Step 1 to bowl and cream with whisk. Add the sugar and whisk
  3. Add the flour and almond powder. Use a rubber spatula to fold mixture together. Don't knead it!
  4. Once the powder is all gone, carefully combine the crumbs together
  5. Put Step 4 on to cellophane on a chopping board, then shape. Leave in fridge for 1-2 hours
  6. Split Step 5 into 30 pieces and shape into balls
  7. Bake Step 6 in 160°C for 20 minutes
  8. Once baked, leave to cool (cooling rack). Sprinkle with sugar (5-10g) while warm. Leave to cool
  9. When cool put in plastic bag and add more sugar (5g). Shake the bag to cover with sugar
